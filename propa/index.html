PROPAGANDA IS A DESIGN STUDIO IN THE ENTERTAINMENT FIELD SPECIALIZING IN FILM, THEATER AND CALLIGRAPHY.
PROPAGANDA DESIGN OUT OF OWN CREATIVE PASSION TO THE SATISFACTION OF OUR CLIENTS.
